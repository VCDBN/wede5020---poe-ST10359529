About us



Vision and Mission statement

Gift of the Givers give hope to the vulnerable in time of need and rebuilding communities for the tomorrow.


Overview

The Gift of the Givers is the largest non-governmental organisation on the African continent.

They common vision of Gift of the Givers is to unite people and make a real difference by helping mankind.


Assistance is provided to human or animal irrespective of race, religion, colour and class.


The organisation has been responsible on delivering goods and services to innumerable people

valued at around R4.5 billion in more then 45 Countries all around the world, including South Africa.

WHAT WE DO

Fast and Active Disaster Response:

We have the capacity to deliver all the materials, which includs:

  • Bedding and blankets
  • Basic food
  • Temporary shelter
  • A wide range of medical supplies
  • Generators for emergency power supply

A Passion for Hunger Alleviation:

  • Hunger is a globe promblem. We distribute over 100 000 food parcels annually and prepare hot meals to help small non-profit organizations on a daily basis.
  • We provide food for animal shelters and for needy farmers during crippling drought events

Promoting Human Development:

All around the world there are high rates of unemployment. Our organization does the following to help:

  • We supply a large quantity of school supplies
  • We have free skills development activities
  • We have a Toll free line
  • We provide life skills training

Contributing to the Healthcare of Communities:

One of your main project is to biuld a hoispital in Northern Syria. We have a clinic currently running with doctors, nurses and mid-wives who work long hours in the field of Somalia.

We have a health care fund for people who are unable to pay their medical bills.


We also have be actively involued in:

  • Building an audiology screening programme in Soshanguve and Pretoria.
  • Funding certain operations
  • Developing a large-scale distribution warehouse for wheelchairs.
